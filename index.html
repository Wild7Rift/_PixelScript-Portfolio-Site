About Me

Hello! I am _PixelScript [Muhammet Ali Yıldırım], a developer and designer who creates projects at the intersection of creativity and technology. I develop a wide range of projects, from pushing the boundaries of music with artificial intelligence to building virtual worlds with 3D modeling and creating fun experiences with game development.

My Projects:

Artificial Intelligence Music: I create original musical compositions using artificial intelligence algorithms. These projects offer an exciting perspective on the future of music.

3D Modeling: I showcase my talents in 3D modeling by designing virtual environments, characters and objects. I use these models for games, animations and virtual reality applications.

Game Development: My passion for game development has led me to produce projects in different game genres. I constantly improve myself in game coding, game design and game mechanics.

Web Development: I create user-friendly and aesthetic websites by producing projects in both web coding and web design fields. These projects combine my mastery of web technologies and my design skills. In every project, I focus on achieving unique and impressive results using innovative approaches and the latest technologies. My passion for continuous learning and self-improvement always leads me to new and exciting projects.

You can learn more about my projects and contact me on this portfolio site.
